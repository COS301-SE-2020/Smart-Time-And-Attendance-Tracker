What is STAT

The Smart Time and Attendance Tracker (STAT) is your personal timekeeper. It is a system designed to assist organisations, particularly small businesses and freelancers, to track time spent on projects and the monetary value surrounding work activities. STAT allow users to track time and attendance based on what they are currently working on in order to minimise costs, whilst maximising productivity.

Why use STAT

STAT is designed to make your job easier. Our features are specially designed to make time and attendance tracking as hands-off as possible. Focus your efforts on developing your business without worrying about keeping track of how you have spent your time.

Features

IOT Integration You can use STAT almost anywhere. Integrate STAT with external devices like cameras to help you track attendance more efficiently. Automatic Tracking Automatically track time spent on your browser and desktop without having to manually start and stop the timer. Predictive Analysis STAT produces graphs that allow you to view how time is or was spent during the course of a project and even predicts future performance. Calendar Syncing STAT can retrieve the data from your calendar and automatically record your presence and track record the relevant time data without you lifting a finger.
More Features

Desktop

Use STAT's desktop app for even more accurate tracking. Simply start the timer and STAT will keep track of the apps you use and automatically create time entries for you
